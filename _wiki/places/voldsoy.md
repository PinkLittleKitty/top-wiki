**Voldsoy** is an island off the coast of Trench in the Twenty One Pilots universe. It serves as a place of refuge and rebirth, playing a critical role in Clancy’s transformation.

## <span class="tape-accent-yellow">Geography</span>

* **Island Location:** Separated from Trench by the Paladin Strait.
* **Inspiration:** Said to resemble the “island of violence” referenced in the song *Migraine*.
> "Tomorrow I'll switch the beat to avoid yesterday's dance / But I'm afraid my concrete shoes will bring me back to this island of violence."
<p class="quote-attribution">- Migraine</p>

## <span class="tape-accent-red">Inhabitants</span>

* **Neds:** The island hosts a large population of Neds. These creatures grant neural expansion via their antlers.
* **Clancy and The Torchbearer:** After escaping the Bishops, Clancy and the Torchbearer wash up on Voldsoy, where Clancy gains the power of psychokinesis.

## <span class="tape-accent-yellow">Role in the Lore</span>

* Serves as a hidden sanctuary for recovery and growth.
* Ned’s Cozy Cabin is located here, indicating a safe, peaceful zone within the chaos of the Trench universe.
